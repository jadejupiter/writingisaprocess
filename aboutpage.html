About Me


Hi there!! My name's Emma, and this is my blog (kinda obvi but yeahh).


I'm a creative (typically fiction) writer and have been writing for a good chunk of my life now. I've come across this problem, however, where getting into a writing "flow" (or maybe groove is a better word) was just hard to do.


When I'd brought this up to some other friends of mine, they'd ask if there was any advice out there for HOW to stay in that flow, or even start a project and stick with it. I'd ended up coming across a couple vidoes, text posts, and personal posts on social medias, but never anything more.


From there, I thought "Why?". Why was there no in-depth writer-made site or forum where writers talked about the process of writing? Because—if anything—writing is 100% a process.


So I decided, "fuck it, i'm going to find a way to make a thing that follows my writing process" and this was born.


This blog is a love-child of a writer wanting to get themself to write, and the process as a helpful little "relatableness" for other writers too! A lot of people write, but usually the creative process is very "free-flowing" and "hard to describe". So why not legitamently follow along with someone going through it?


Finally, This website was coded and completed by me !!
